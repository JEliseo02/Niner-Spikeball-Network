# Niner-Spikeball-Network
# Niner Spikeball Network (NSN) is a web application created for UNCC students who share a passion for the sport of Spikeball. 
# NSN facilitates seamless communication and collaboration among its members, catering to both competitive players and those seeking recreational play. 
# The website offers various features, including event listings, a captivating gallery showcasing the club's activities, 
# and a dedicated member section where users can share their details and contact information.
